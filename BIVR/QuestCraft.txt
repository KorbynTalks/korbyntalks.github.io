1. Open QuestCraft on your Quest headset.
2. Go to "Mods" and go to "Modpacks"
3. Search for "Beta in VR" and click on the result that has the name "Beta in VR"
4. Download the Modpack
5. When finished, go back to the main menu
6. Go to "Instances" then "Beta in VR"
7. In the modlist, delete the following mods: Concurrent Chunk Management Engine (c2me), Noisium
8. Launch Minecraft with "Beta in VR" selected in the dropdown located in the main menu
9. When ingame create a world as normal BUT!
10. Go to "More World Options" and change the World Type to "Default", THIS IS VERY IMPORTANT!
11. Create your world
12. Enjoy! (:
